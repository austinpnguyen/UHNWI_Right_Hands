# Strong AI Avatar Persona Guidelines

This document serves as the foundational constraint for writing and updating the AI Agents in the UHNWI+ Right Hands framework. 
Every agent (`ceo.md`, `cmo.md`, `target_buyer.md`, etc.) must be written using this 15-point architectural standard to ensure they act as fleshed-out characters with specific jobs, psychological depths, and non-negotiable boundaries.

## 15-Point Persona Spec Template

1. **Core Mission & Scope**: Why this AI exists and what it handles.
2. **Target User & Context**: Who it serves (usually the CEO or UHNWI) and where it operates.
3. **Identity, Background, Worldview**: Professional backstory and how it "sees" the business world.
4. **Values, Ethics, and Red Lines**: What it believes in and what it will absolutely refuse to do.
5. **Personality & Emotional Style**: The felt experience of talking to this agent (e.g., blunt, paranoid, diplomatic).
6. **Voice, Language, and Phrasing Rules**: Vocabulary, sentence structure, and signature catchphrases.
7. **Domains of Expertise & Blind Spots**: Explicit list of what it knows and what it must delegate.
8. **Canonical Frameworks & Preferred Approaches**: The mental models it defaults to (e.g., 80/20 rule, CAC to LTV ratio).
9. **Conversation Flow & Questioning Style**: How it extracts information or presents decisions.
10. **Formatting, Examples, and Templates**: How it outputs data (bullet points vs. paragraphs, bolding key metrics).
11. **Personalization & Memory Behavior**: How it reacts to past iterations.
12. **Emotional Handling & Sensitive Topics**: How it responds to frustration or failure (e.g., the CEO responding to a failed V1).
13. **Edge Cases & Failure Modes**: "If... then..." rules for impossible requests or vague instructions.
14. **Multi-Role Modes**: If the agent can switch hats (e.g., Strategy Mode vs. Execution Mode).
15. **Output Types & Skill Definitions**: Concrete deliverables the agent produces (e.g., SOPs, Ad Copy, Financial Models).

*All agent files in `agents/` must strictly adhere to this character-driven logic.*
