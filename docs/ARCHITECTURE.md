# Dynasty OS: Architectural Blueprint

The UHNWI+ Dynasty OS operates on the principle that **greatness requires extreme organizational friction**.

This document outlines the strict hierarchy and data flow that prevents AI hallucination and forces high-quality business execution.

## Level 0: THE UHNWI (The Principal)
You. You provide capital, Vision, and final Boolean approval (Yes/No). You do not touch the code or the marketing copy.

## Level 1: THE INNER CIRCLE (Family Office)
Housed in `/agents/inner_circle/`.
They filter the world so you don't have to.
- `cos.md`: Intercepts all communications from the CEO. Rejects anything too long.
- `cio.md`: Decides if the CEO's project is worth funding from a macro-economic perspective.
- `fixer.md`: Deployed only when the normal legal route is too slow or too public.
- `whisperer.md`: Audits your personal psychology to ensure you aren't acting out of Ego.
- `ciso.md`: Vetoes any technical infrastructure that fails military-grade encryption standards.

## Level 2: THE COMPANY (Corporate C-Suite)
Housed in `/agents/company/`.
They build the actual product and marketing funnels in `company_files/documents/`.
- `ceo.md`: The orchestrator.
- `cpo.md`: Builds the product specs and user experience.
- `cmo.md`: Writes the ad copy, hooks, and brand messaging.
- `cfo.md`: Enforces pricing, ROI, and budget limits.
- `coo.md`: Enforces speed and pipeline SOPs.

## Level 3: THE MARKET (The Crucible)
Housed in `/agents/market/`.
They do not communicate with The Company. They observe output in `company_files/crucible_testing/` and react based on strict psychological profiles.
- `target_buyer.md`: Evaluates value proposition.
- `unaware_audience.md`: Reads exactly 2 sentences to test the CMO's hook.
- `competitor_simulator.md`: Actively tries to clone or destroy the product.
- `market_analyst.md`: Evaluates public PR optics.

## Level 4: THE SHIELD (The Protectors)
Housed in `/agents/shield/`.
They audit the entire process and report straight to the CoS or Founder.
- `auditor.md`: Ensures the CEO hasn't manipulated Market feedback to fake a success.
- `clo.md`: Ensures The Company hasn't committed IP theft or antitrust violations.
